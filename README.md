# Religionly

> Cross-cultural HR training platform for international workforces

**Live:** [religionly.de](https://religionly.de)

---

## Overview

Religionly helps HR departments, expatriates, and business travelers understand world religions, cultural etiquette, and country-specific religious practices. Built for organizations managing internationally mobile employees and cross-cultural teams.

## Key Features

- **Interactive World Map** — country-religion data visualization
- **Religion Profiles** — detailed guides with cultural and historical context
- **Cultural Etiquette Guides** — country-specific dos and don'ts for business
- **HR Training Resource** — structured content for intercultural management programs

## Tech Stack

`Vite` `React` `TypeScript` `Tailwind CSS`
